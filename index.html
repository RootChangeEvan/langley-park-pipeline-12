One shared view of the work — for everyone involved.
This Progress Pipeline gives CASA, the Coalition, the Steering Committee, and Root Change a single place to see where the work stands, who owns what, and what needs a decision — without emails or status calls.
  • See where you are. The journey bar at the top shows all four phases at a glance. Click any phase to jump straight to it.
  • Track what's in progress. Each milestone shows its owner, status, and outputs. Click to expand for full detail.
  • Know what needs a decision. Decision points are flagged in gold and collected in the sidebar — so sign-offs are never buried in the workplan.
  • Follow the activity. The sidebar tracks recent updates so anyone joining mid-process can get oriented fast.
